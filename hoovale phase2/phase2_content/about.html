Our Story

HOOVALE was founded in {{ site.establishment_year }} in Jaipur, Rajasthan — India's craft and manufacturing heartland — with a simple goal: deliver high-quality wall clocks at honest factory prices, without the middleman markups that dominate the Indian clock market.

Over the years we've grown from a small workshop into a full-scale manufacturing facility producing thousands of clocks every month. Our customer base spans 150+ cities across India — from large clock wholesalers in Delhi's Sadar Bazar to luxury hotel chains in Mumbai, IT corporates in Bangalore, and real estate developers gifting flat owners across the country.

Our success comes from a simple promise: direct factory pricing, consistent quality, and on-time delivery. We don't trade. We don't outsource. We make every clock ourselves — and that's why our prices are honest and our quality is reliable.
